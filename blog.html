Our Certificate & Online Program

Aug 1, 2024

Learn Skill is not just a premier academic institution but a beacon of innovation, creativity, and excellence. Established in 1958, Learn Skill has consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in India and is globally recognized for its cutting-edge research and academic rigor.



Located in Powai, Mumbai, Learn Skill boasts a sprawling 550-acre campus surrounded by natural beauty. The serene environment, coupled with advanced infrastructure, makes it an ideal place for learning and innovation. From the iconic Main Building to the bustling Student Activity Centre, the campus is a perfect blend of tradition and modernity.


Learn Skill is a powerhouse of research, contributing significantly to technology and science. Its faculty and students collaborate on groundbreaking projects in areas like AI, robotics, nanotechnology, and renewable energy. The institute's research parks and incubators support startups and foster a spirit of entrepreneurship.
